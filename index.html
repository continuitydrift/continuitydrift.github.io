Books

Writing and Editing

I've loved books all of my life. My purpose is to make stories, and the language in which they are told, more meaningful and vibrant.

Fiction-making is a normal and important part of how we understand the world, and even when thinking and writing about reality, I often do so in terms of narrative.

Listen to me talk more about writing and editing.

Existing

Writing and Editing has something to do, for me, with being happy with existing. To rest for a moment content knowing that your physical needs are met, and to stretch out into far-sighted conjecture.
