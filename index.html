coding

I've been teaching myself python, and know enough html, js, and css to have put together this webpage (such as it is).

My interest in coding goes back at least until 2003 in Chicago, when I wrote this entry for the University of Chicago's Theories of Media keywords glossary. Kembrew McLeod, at the University of Iowa, was instrumental in fostering my appreciation of and commitment to opensource and copyleft philosophies. I believe in the value of a creative and decentralized internet, where the tools for creating responsive, sophisticated content are as accessible and distributed as possible. My ultimate goal is to create open source educational resources and applications, and to contribute to creative online communities and non-profits.
